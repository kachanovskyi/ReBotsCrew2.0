What is BotsCrew?

A Chatbot is a virtual assistant powered by rules and algorithms that lets your customers interact with your business through several messaging platforms. These Chatbots are intuitive to use and can be implemented into several fields of business such as e-commerce, entertainment, news, customer support, delivery services or corporate information exchange.
