Technologies

Introduction

The RTS Group, established in 1989, is one of Europe's largest after-sales management and most powerful service providers for IT and multi-media equipment. With more than 1,300 employees, the company has established itself at the locations throughout Germany with repairs based on industrial scale, technical support and solutions relating to spare parts management including the associated logistics. And all the signs point to further growth, since Tec InStore have been part of the Media-Saturn Group since October 2015, making Tec InStore Europe's largest electronics retailer. Involved in the RTS Group, Tec Repair GmbH specializes in a round-premium service and fast on-site service for small businesses and business customers.

Challange

As a big repair center of the phones, Tec InStore always had a lot of requests from their customers. They were describing their problems with the phone, asking typical questions to the customer support center. It took a lot of money and affords to satisfy every customer and answer on his question. And it was hard to scale.

Solution

This chatbot, developed by us, uses our NLP tools and is able to answer the questions that concern the customers of Tec InStore the most often. For example, how much will it cost to replace the screen on Iphone 7? How much time will it take to upgrade the software on Samsung Galaxy S7? While developing the bot we faced with the problem that people often make mistakes in the keywords. This prompted us to accept the challenge of understanding and correcting errors, which we have successfully coped.

Also, the chatbot is very flexible and open to the extension without any additional interventions of the programmer. We have developed the convenient dashboard that makes it easy to manage the content of the chatbot.

Result

We have created a chatbot for FB messenger that help Tec InStore customers to get the immidiate answer to their questions regarding phone`s repapair. After one month of usage chatbot had a 80% success rate , more than 1500 people has requested a help from the chatbot.
