IT Sales/Marketing manager

Lviv, Ukraine

Overview

What is our definition of a superstar salesperson? Someone who:

  • Can establish rapport with anyone
  • Can earn a client's trust in a short period of time
  • Exudes self confidence
  • Doesn't give up
  • Listens more than talk
  • Learns every day and is committed to personal development

Required skills

  • Fluent English
  • Strong communication skills via email or over the phone. This is a must
  • Highly motivated and dedicated with an "ownership" mindset
  • Dedicated to an excellent customer service experience
  • Skilled at building relationships with people
  • Team player
  • Desire for self-development
  • An experience of living/working in English speaking countries will be a plus
  • Can be a student

Responsibilities

  • Sales planning
  • Call prospective customers by phone
  • Adjust sales scripts to better target the needs and interests of specific individuals
  • Establishing contact with potential customers (chat & Skype)
  • Sales of chat bot development (using Upwork, LinkedIn, etc.)
  • Holding negotiations with long-term clients
  • Marketing activities (market research, content management, website management, SMM, etc.)
  • Active participation in marketing events

We offer:

  • Friendly, young and outstanding team
  • USA(San Francisco, Houston, New York ), Singapore and Europe business trips
  • Comfortable working space
  • Competitive compensation with bonuses
  • Full-time work (flexible working hours)
  • Team-buildings in mountains or abroad
  • Opportunity to develop your skills
  • Possibility to participate in different courses, events, training sessions, seminars
  • Tons of challenges and hard work
  • 4 working weeks paid vacation
